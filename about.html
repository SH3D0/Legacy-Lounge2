About Us

Legacy Lounge is a distinguished dining destination renowned for its exceptional culinary experiences and luxurious ambiance. Situated in the vibrant city of Colombo, Sri Lanka, Legacy Lounge has carved a niche for itself as a premier restaurant offering an exquisite blend of traditional and contemporary cuisine. With an emphasis on quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction, Legacy Lounge is the perfect place for food enthusiasts seeking an unforgettable dining experience.

Vision and Mission

Vision:

To be the leading restaurant in Colombo, celebrated for its culinary excellence, innovative dining experiences, and exceptional customer service.

Mission:

  • To offer a diverse menu that combines the finest local and international flavors.
  • To provide a dining environment that is both luxurious and welcoming.
  • To maintain the highest standards of quality in food, service, and ambiance.
  • To continually innovate and adapt to the evolving tastes and preferences of our customers.
  • To foster a culture of excellence and teamwork among our staff.

Colombo Locations

Legacy Lounge operates multiple locations in Colombo to cater to its growing clientele. Each location is strategically situated to provide easy access and a unique dining experience:

Legacy Lounge - Colombo Fort:

Located in the heart of the city, this flagship branch is perfect for business lunches, romantic dinners, and social gatherings.

Legacy Lounge - Cinnamon Gardens:

Nestled in one of Colombo’s most prestigious neighborhoods, this branch offers a serene and sophisticated atmosphere ideal for intimate dining and special occasions.

Legacy Lounge - Colombo 07:

This location boasts a contemporary design and is a favorite among young professionals and families looking for a chic dining experience.

Service Types

History and Management

Legacy Lounge was established in 2010 by a group of passionate restaurateurs who envisioned creating a dining destination that would set new standards in the Colombo food scene. Over the years, the restaurant has grown from a single outlet to multiple locations, thanks to its unwavering commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

The management team at Legacy Lounge comprises seasoned professionals with extensive experience in the hospitality and culinary industries. Their collective expertise and dedication have been instrumental in the restaurant’s success. Key members of the management team include:

Mr. Dulaj Nethmika (CEO):

With over two decades of experience in the hospitality industry, Mr. Perera leads Legacy Lounge with a focus on strategic growth and innovation.

Chef Tharindu Dilshan (Executive Chef):

A culinary maestro, Chef Tharindu is the creative force behind Legacy Lounge’s acclaimed menu, bringing a fusion of traditional Sri Lankan and international flavors to the table.

Mr. Heshan Edirisinha (Operations Manager):

Mr. Heshan Edirisinha the seamless operation of all Legacy Lounge locations, maintaining the highest standards of service and quality.

Conclusion

Legacy Lounge continues to be a beacon of culinary excellence in Colombo, offering an unparalleled dining experience that combines exquisite food, luxurious ambiance, and exceptional service. Whether you are looking for a place to celebrate a special occasion, enjoy a business lunch, or simply indulge in a gourmet meal, Legacy Lounge promises to exceed your expectations.
